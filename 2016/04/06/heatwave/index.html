Heatwave

Apr 6 2016
Tags: None.
Categories: None.

It climbed into the 80s today in the Bay. And all I could think was I would rather be anywhere else but here.

Now I’m sitting in the dark with a box fan in the window and a cat taking all the fresh cool air. Well one of them at least, the other one has never seen a box fan before so he’s hiding under the bed. It’s a tough call, but I think I’d still rather be somewhere else besides here.

It’s supposed to rain here this weekend. I love the rain. I will sit on my bed and listen to it tap against my window. But in reality I will probably put my headphones in and watch stupid things on the internet. Just to tick by the hours. I don’t know where else to go but here.
